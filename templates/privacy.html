Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2024

1. Introduction

QuakeMeUp ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our earthquake monitoring service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Location data (with your explicit permission)
  • Device information
  • Usage statistics
  • Notification preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide earthquake alerts and notifications
  • Improve our service
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Ensure service security

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your information:

  • We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties
  • We do not share your location data with any external services or companies
  • Your data is used solely for the purpose of providing earthquake monitoring and alert services
  • The only exception is when required by law or to protect our legal rights

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Your location data is stored securely and is only used for the purpose of providing earthquake alerts.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of notifications
  • Modify your preferences

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@quakemeup.com
